About Us

Our Story

Yorkshire Art House is situated in the heart of Swaledale in the beautiful village of Reeth. Jo opened the gallery as a collaborative space for like-minded artists to share their art and crafts. Her work is inspired by the stunning Dales’ landscapes, the amazing colours and patterns she experiences on her walks and the feelings they evoke. YAH is a space for artists to show and sell their work and for visitors to drop in for a browse and a chat.

About the Artist

Jo studied at UCLAN in the early 1980s as a Fine Art Printmaker, specialising in etching and lithography. She taught art in after school clubs in London and has designed and created sets for children’s theatre productions in Zakynthos Greece. She now focuses on landscapes in a variety of mediums, such as watercolours, acrylics, pastels, etching and aquatints, and wet-felting. Many artists inspire her work, but most notably are David Hockney’s Yorkshire landscapes and Norman Ackroyd’s aquatints.

Our Community

YAH runs regular classes for all ages and abilities, taught by Jo and invited artists. Participants are invited to show their work in YAH exhibitions which are run monthly.
