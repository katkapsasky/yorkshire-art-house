About Us

Our Story

The Yorkshire Art House is a small, family-run gallery located in the village of Reeth, nestled amongst the rolling hills of the North Yorkshire Dales. Our gallery welcomes locals and tourists alike and we have a number of paintings and prints on display in store and online for visitors to enjoy. Our art work is inspired by the nature that surrounds us, from the dramatic shapes of the tumbling fells to the vibrant greens of the fields amongst them.

About the Artist

All the artwork on display is created by Jo Thomas, who has spent the past 10 years living in Reeth. Jo has always been passionate about art.
